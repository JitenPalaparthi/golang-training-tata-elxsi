Nirmala Sitharaman Education: The current Finance Minister of India, Nirmala Sitharaman, 
is the Indian Finance Minister who has studied at Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU). 
Sitharaman completed her Master of Arts (MA) in Economics and M.Phil. in Economics from JNU in the early 1980s (around 1984).
 While JNU has produced many prominent figures in Indian politics and governance, 
 Nirmala Sitharaman is the only person who has served as the Union Finance Minister of India and studied at JNU.

 Sitharaman is in news today as she presented the Union Budget 2026-27. She is first Finance Minister to present the Union Budget for a record ninth time.
